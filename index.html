Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this conference for?

▼

The Growth Experience is designed for professionals, entrepreneurs, and individuals at any career stage who are committed to meaningful personal and professional development. Whether you're just starting out or already leading, there's value for you.

Is this a virtual or in-person event?

▼

The Growth Experience 2026 is an in-person event held at the Conference Center in Excellence City, June 15-17, 2026. The in-person format is intentional—the energy, networking, and connections are core to the experience.

Will recordings be available?

▼

Selected keynotes and sessions will be recorded and made available to Standard and VIP ticket holders within 30 days of the conference. The full interactive experience, however, is designed for in-person attendance.

What's the refund policy?

▼

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. From day 31 until day 1 of the conference, we offer exchange to a future event or 50% credit. No refunds are issued for no-shows. Contact us at info@tge26.com for any changes.

Can I upgrade my ticket after purchase?

▼

Yes! You can upgrade from Early Bird to Standard or VIP, or from Standard to VIP at any time. Simply email info@tge26.com with your upgrade request, and we'll adjust your ticket and send an invoice for the difference.

What's included in the digital resource library?

▼

Standard and VIP tickets include access to: session slides, speaker resources, action plan templates, worksheets, reading lists, tool recommendations, and exclusive guides created by our speakers for attendees.

Do you have group discounts?

▼

Yes! Groups of 5 or more receive 10% off. Contact our team at info@tge26.com with the names of your group members, and we'll set up your group registration.

What about accessibility and accommodations?

▼

We're committed to accessibility. The venue is wheelchair accessible, and we offer accommodations for various needs. Please reach out to info@tge26.com at least 2 weeks before the event to discuss your specific requirements.

Can I bring a guest or colleague?

▼

Absolutely! If you're interested in registering colleagues or friends, they can purchase their own ticket or you can contact us about group arrangements. The more people attending, the richer the experience.
